TRAINING PROGRAMS

Beginner (White–Yellow Belt)

– Build fitness, flexibility, and basics: stances, simple kicks/blocks/punches, light sparring, conditioning, stretching.

Intermediate (Green–Blue Belt)

– Improve speed, power, combos: advanced kicks, poomsae, sparring, defense drills, strength and flexibility training.

Advanced (Red–Black Belt)

– Mastery and leadership: specialized kicks, complex combos, black belt poomsae, full sparring, breaking techniques, tactical drills, instructor skills.

Specialization

– Competition sparring, demonstration team skills, and self-defense applications.
